Our plans

At RAZZ, custumer satistafction is our number one priority, and thats why we offer different plans depending on our custurme's need. Our plans are:

Basic

Meet our first deal, the basic package. With this package, you can have acces to 10 Full Charging Cycles. With this package, you can only have acces to our services in ottawa, but at anytime. This package only includes magnetic charging.

Standard

The second deal that we have to offer is the Standard package. With thi package, you can have acces to 30 Full Charging cycles. This package comes with the possibility of accessing our services anywhere in Canada. This package only includes magnetic charging.

Super

And finally, we offer you our final and last deal, the Super deal. This package is the maximum that we can offer to our costumers. Not only do we offer 50 Full charging Cycles, accessible all around the world, but we also add a 5 GB Data plan.
